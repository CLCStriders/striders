On a cool and overcast late spring morning, seven CLC Striders made the short journey east to compete in the Sherborne 5-mile trail race around the beautiful grounds of the Sherborne National Trust estate.
 
Just two days after her storming run in the Bourton Mile, Helen Knight led the club home with a top 10 finish to bag 1st place in her LV40 category and 7th overall in a time of 34:13. Fiona Brady-Miller earned 1st place in her LV50 category in a time of 37:37. Next came the Weager sisters – Nicola in 41:20 and Hannah in 43:12. Deb Cartlidge clocked 45:19 for 2nd LV50, Hifsa O’Kelly finished in 46:05, Helen Rawcliffe registered 47:13 and Jill Harsent was 2nd in her LV60 category in 47:17.
