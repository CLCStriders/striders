In the first local track meet of the year, three Striders put themselves to the test over the 3000m distance. Making his track debut, Pete Duffy set a new club category record in a time of 9:40.10, earning himself a VM35 gold medal in the County Champs. Rachel Vines ran a time of 11:54.05 to become County Champion in her age category, and Joel Freeland earned himself a silver County medal for his VM40 category with a time of 11:55.32.
